# **CASE STUDY 1**

# **How We Scaled a Kitchen Brand from $9K to $82K Monthly Revenue in 6 Months**

## **Client Background**

The client was a **private label kitchen tools brand** selling a silicone baking accessory on Amazon.

Despite having a good product, the brand struggled to gain traction in a competitive market dominated by well-established sellers.

Initial Situation:

* Monthly Revenue: **$9,300**

* ACOS: **54%**

* Conversion Rate: **8.2%**

* Very poor organic keyword rankings

The seller approached our agency to **revive their listing and build a scalable growth system.**

---

## **The Challenges**

The brand faced several major issues:

* Poor listing SEO and weak keyword coverage

* Low click-through rate due to weak product images

* High ACOS with inefficient PPC campaign structure

* No ranking strategy for important keywords

* Weak A+ content and poor brand positioning

---

## **Our Strategy**

Our team implemented a **full Amazon growth framework** combining listing optimization and PPC scaling.

Key strategic improvements included:

* Deep **keyword research using Helium10 & Amazon search data**

* Complete **listing SEO optimization**

* High-converting **infographic images redesign**

* Creation of **A+ brand content**

* Full **PPC campaign restructuring**

* **Keyword ranking campaigns** for high-volume search terms

* **Competitor pricing analysis**

---

## **Execution Steps**

1. Conducted advanced keyword research (2,000+ keywords analyzed)

2. Rewrote listing title, bullets, and backend search terms

3. Redesigned listing images to highlight benefits and differentiation

4. Built a structured PPC framework (Auto \+ Exact \+ Phrase \+ Product targeting)

5. Launched keyword ranking campaigns

6. Weekly PPC optimization and bid adjustments

7. Ongoing conversion optimization

---

## **Results**

Within **6 months**, the brand experienced massive growth.

### **Before**

* Monthly Revenue: **$9,300**

* ACOS: **54%**

* Conversion Rate: **8.2%**

* Top Keyword Rank: **Page 4**

### **After**

* Monthly Revenue: **$82,000**

* ACOS: **26%**

* Conversion Rate: **18.4%**

* Top Keyword Rank: **Top 5 positions**

---

## **Client Testimonial**

*"Their strategy completely transformed our Amazon business. Within months our sales skyrocketed and our ads became profitable. The team knows exactly how to scale Amazon brands."*

— Founder, Kitchen Brand

---

## **Key Takeaway**

**Combining listing conversion optimization with a structured PPC ranking strategy is the fastest way to scale an Amazon product in competitive niches.**
